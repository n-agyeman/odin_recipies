Lasagna

Description

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Ingredients

  1. Beef: This lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use ground turkey for lighter option.\n
  2. Spaghetti sauce: Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti sauce.
  3. Cheeses: You'll need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  4. Eggs: Eggs help bind the cheese mixture togetehr. Plus, htey lend moisture and richness.
  5. Seasonings: Season the easy lasgna with dried parsley, salt and black pepper.
  6. lasagna noodles: Of course, you'll need lasagna noodles!
  7. Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edgesof hte baking dish before baking
